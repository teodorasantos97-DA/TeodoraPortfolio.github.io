Massachusetts Education Performance Dashboard

Interactive Tableau dashboard analysing 1,861 schools and 953,748 students.

Key Insights

  • Large performance differences exist across districts, especially in graduation rates and 4th grade math proficiency.
  • Socioeconomic disadvantage is strongly associated with lower college attendance outcomes.
  • Higher early math performance is linked to stronger long-term outcomes across the system.
  • Class size alone does not appear to be the main driver of college attendance.
